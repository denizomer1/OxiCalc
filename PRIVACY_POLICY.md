# Privacy Policy for OxiCalc

**Effective Date:** June 25, 2024

OxiCalc ("we," "us," or "our") operates the OxiCalc mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## 1. Data Collection
OxiCalc is designed to be a private and local-first application. 
- **Personal Data:** We **do not collect** any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
- **Usage Data:** We **do not track** your usage patterns, calculation history, or device information on any external servers.

## 2. Local Storage
All data generated by the App, including your calculation history and settings (theme preferences), is stored **locally on your device**. This data never leaves your device unless you manually perform a system backup or share it using your device's native features.

## 3. Third-Party Services
OxiCalc does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking SDKs that transmit your data to third parties.

## 4. Open Source
OxiCalc is an open-source project. You can review the full source code on our GitHub repository to verify our privacy claims and security practices.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository issues page.
